World's Best Lasagna

One of the most famous dishes of Italy comes packed with a meaty sauce and
nice cheese variety.

Prep Time:

30 mins

Cook Time:

2 hrs 30 mins

Additional Time:

15 mins

Total Time:

3 hrs 15 mins

Servings:

12

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Make the Meat Sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the Layers, then top with remaining Parmesan.

How long to cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
