Chili Sin Carne

Mexican Chili is one of those dishes lightening up your mood after a long
and exhausting day. That one's a plant-based recipe with simple ingredients,
that you might want to look out for in your storage. It's easy, fast and still
so delicious.

Ingredients

  • Onions and Garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Chili for some spiciness
  • Tomatoes -hacked-
  • Kidney Beans
  • Corn
  • Carrots
  • Lentils, Chickpeas or some other 'meat-substitute of choice'
  • Rice

  1. Preparations

    Mince garlic, onions and carrots and soak the granulate in water for needed
    amount of time. If you're using tofu, you can skip this step.

  2. Cooking

    Roast the minced veggies altogether with the vegan meat in a pan with some
    vegetable oil. Add chili, pepper, tomato-paste and stir-fry shortly. Afterwards
    you can add tomatoes, broth, kidney beans, corn as well as salt, pepper or sugar.

    Let it simmer for 20-30 minutes on low heat, meanwhile cook the rice.
    Lastly, give it some taste-testing and season to your liking.
    Enjoy!
