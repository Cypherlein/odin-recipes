Raspberry Chocolate Cake

This beautiful vegan Raspberry Chocolate Cake is made with healthy ingredients and natural sweetness. It's therefore completely without butter, cream, cream cheese nor eggs.

Ingredients

  • For the Chocolate-Base

    • Almonds
    • Oats
    • Cocoa powder
    • Salt
    • Soft dates
    • Maple Syrup
    • Coconut Oil

  • For the Raspberry-Filling

    • Coconut Cream
    • Frozen Raspberries
    • Raw Cane Sugar
    • Agar Agar
    • Cornstarch
    • Vanilla

How to Make the Cake

  1. Create the Chocolate-Base

    Blend the nuts and oats, mix it with cocoa powder, salt, dates, maple syrup and liquid coconut cream 15-20 Minutes into preheated oven (300°F), then let cool down

  2. Raspberry-Filling

    Thaw the raspberries in a pot, mix cornstarch and agar-agar with 1/4 cup of water. Press raspberries through colander and catch the juice. Combine it with the starch-agar-mixture and cook for about 2 minutes. Fill it into your base and let it cool down in the freezer.

  3. Decorate

    Before serving, take fresh rasp- or strawberries, sprinkles or anything else you like and put it on this beautiful pink-reddish cream. Enjoy!
